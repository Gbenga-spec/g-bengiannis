Giannis Antetokounmpo

Giannis Sina Ugo Antetokunmpo born Adétòkunbọ̀;[b] December 6, 1994 is a Greek-Nigerian professional basketball player for the Milwaukee Bucks of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Antetokounmpo's country of origin, in addition to his size, speed, strength, and ball-handling skills have earned him the nickname "Greek Freak". Antetokounmpo is widely regarded as one of the greatest power forwards and one of the greatest European of all time.
